| V              | Potential energy density.                                        | kJ/m² ; V/(ρgd²)    |
| E              | Total energy density E = T + V.                                  | kJ/m²               |
| F              | Wave power (energy flux).                                        | kW/m ;              |
|                |                                                                  | F/(ρg³ᐟ²d⁵ᐟ²)ㅤ      |
